Acceptable Use Policy — Version 4
Effective: 2026-04-01

Section 1. Data Classification
All data handled by the organization is classified into one of four tiers:
Public, Internal, Confidential, and Restricted. Tier classification must be
applied at the time of data creation or ingestion.

Section 2. Encryption Requirements
All Personally Identifiable Information (PII) and all Confidential or
Restricted data must be encrypted at rest using AES-256 or equivalent.
Data in transit must be protected using TLS 1.3 or higher.

Section 3. External Sharing
Public and Internal tier data may be shared externally with appropriate
approvals. Confidential and Restricted data may not be shared externally
except under a written exception authorized by Legal and the data steward.

Section 4. Retention
Personally Identifiable Information is retained for no longer than the
operational need plus 30 days. Audit logs are retained for 7 years.
